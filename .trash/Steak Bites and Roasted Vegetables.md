# Sheet Pan Garlic Butter Steak Bites & Vegetables

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 lb sirloin steak, cubed
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Garlic
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] 1 head broccoli
- [ ] 1 lb baby potatoes
- [ ] 1 red onion
- [ ] Paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, rosemary
- [ ] Salt & pepper

## Method
Season steak and vegetables separately, roast on one sheet pan (steak one side, veg the other), 400°F ~25 min.

## Prep style
Raw-prep, freeze, thaw & cook.
