# Vegetable Beef Soup

Cubed chuck roast or beef stew meat — no ground beef, no tomato juice.

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 lb chuck roast or beef stew meat, cubed
- [ ] 4 cups beef broth
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 2-3 celery stalks
- [ ] 3-4 carrots
- [ ] 1.5 lb potatoes
- [ ] Frozen peas
- [ ] Garlic
- [ ] Salt & pepper

## Method
Sear beef, transfer to crockpot with broth and aromatics, cook low 8 hrs. Add potatoes/carrots partway through, peas at the end.

## Prep style
Crockpot meal — cook, portion, freeze.
