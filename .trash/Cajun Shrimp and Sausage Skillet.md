# Cajun Shrimp and Sausage Skillet

Cast iron skillet, ~10 minutes, done on the stovetop.

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 lb shrimp, peeled/deveined
- [ ] 1 package andouille or smoked chicken/pork sausage
- [ ] 3 bell peppers (mixed colors)
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] Garlic
- [ ] Cajun seasoning
- [ ] Avocado or olive oil
- [ ] Salt & pepper
- [ ] Side: rice or extra potatoes

## Method
Sear sausage, add peppers/onion, season with cajun spice, add shrimp last until pink and opaque.

## Prep style
Raw-prep, freeze, thaw & cook.
