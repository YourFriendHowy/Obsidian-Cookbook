# Chicken and Dumplings

Big dumplings, not keto — Pillsbury Grands butter tube biscuits dropped whole into the simmering broth.

## Ingredients
- [ ] Chicken or turkey breast
- [ ] Carrots, celery, onion, garlic
- [ ] Chicken broth
- [ ] Pillsbury Grands butter biscuits (2 per serving)
- [ ] Salt & pepper, poultry seasoning

## Method
Simmer chicken/turkey with broth and mirepoix until tender, shred. Drop whole biscuits into simmering broth, cover, cook until dumplings are cooked through (don't lift the lid).

## Notes
~180-190 cal per raw Grands biscuit — budget 2 per serving (~370 cal) in calorie tracking. Lean on the vegetable/protein ratio to balance it out.

## Prep style
Best made fresh — dumplings don't freeze/reheat well.
