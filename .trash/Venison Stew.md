# Slow Cooker Venison Stew

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 lb venison stew meat
- [ ] Flour
- [ ] Beef broth
- [ ] Potatoes
- [ ] Carrots
- [ ] Onion
- [ ] Garlic
- [ ] Salt & pepper

## Method
Coat venison in seasoned flour, brown, transfer to crockpot with broth, vegetables and aromatics. Cook low 8 hrs.

## Prep style
Crockpot meal — cook, portion, freeze.
