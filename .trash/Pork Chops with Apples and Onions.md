# Skillet-to-Oven Pork Chops with Apples & Onions

Cast-iron skillet sear, then finish in the oven — matches your equipment exactly.

## Ingredients
- [ ] 4-6 pork chops (bone-in or boneless)
- [ ] 2 apples (tart variety, e.g. Granny Smith)
- [ ] 1-2 onions
- [ ] Garlic
- [ ] Fresh or dried rosemary
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Olive oil
- [ ] Flour (optional, for pan sauce)
- [ ] Salt & pepper

## Method
Sear chops in cast iron, remove. Soften onions and apples in the same pan, nestle chops back in, finish in the oven at 350°F.

## Prep style
Raw-prep, freeze, thaw & cook.
