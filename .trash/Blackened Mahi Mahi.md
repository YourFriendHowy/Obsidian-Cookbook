# Blackened Mahi Mahi + Side

Buy fresh, cook same day — don't freeze raw.

## Ingredients
- [ ] Mahi mahi fillets (fresh)
- [ ] Butter
- [ ] Onion powder, garlic powder, smoked paprika
- [ ] Cayenne (optional)
- [ ] Salt & pepper
- [ ] Lemon
- [ ] Side: rice or roasted potatoes + a veggie (green beans or broccoli)

## Method
Coat fillets in blackening seasoning, sear in hot cast iron skillet with butter, 3-4 min per side.

## Prep style
Fresh only — cook same day, no freezing raw.
