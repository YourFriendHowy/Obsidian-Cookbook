# Sausage & Pepper Jack Croissant Sandwich

Sunday prep, weekday reheat.

## Ingredients
- [ ] Breakfast sausage (for patties)
- [ ] Pepper jack cheese slices
- [ ] Raw croissant dough (bakery-fresh if possible, or canned/tube)

## Method
Sunday: cook sausage patties fully. Assemble with pepper jack cheese inside raw croissant dough, seal, freeze.
Weeknight: move to fridge to thaw overnight. Bake per dough package's time + 2-3 min extra (since it's cold from the fridge) for a fresh-baked croissant with hot filling.

## Prep style
Sunday batch prep, freeze, fridge-thaw overnight, bake fresh each morning.
