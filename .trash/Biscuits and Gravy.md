# Biscuits and Gravy

Savory breakfast exception — every couple of weeks.

## Ingredients
- [ ] Biscuit mix
- [ ] Breakfast sausage
- [ ] Flour
- [ ] Milk

## Method
Bake biscuits. Brown sausage, make a roux with flour, whisk in milk to make gravy, season generously. Serve gravy over split biscuits.

## Prep style
Best made fresh, occasional (not weekly).
