# Turkey Vegetable Soup

Whole turkey breast(s), slow cooked and shredded — not ground. Skip the egg noodles or sub potatoes/rice instead of wheat pasta.

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 whole turkey breasts (bone-in or boneless)
- [ ] 7 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- [ ] 3 carrots
- [ ] 3 celery stalks
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 2 garlic cloves
- [ ] Bay leaves
- [ ] Italian seasoning
- [ ] Black pepper
- [ ] Potatoes or rice (instead of noodles)

## Method
Slow cook turkey breast with broth, aromatics, and vegetables until tender (about 4-5 hrs high / 6-7 hrs low). Shred turkey with two forks, return to pot. Add potatoes or rice near the end instead of noodles.

## Prep style
Crockpot meal — cook, portion, freeze.
